Let's get to know each other

Hi!, let me tell you some things about us . After graduating as architects we found in ArchViz, the perfect joint between our profession, and our passion. We traveled around the world almost for one year and we get extensive experience that shaped our view of the architecture, gave a huge a dose of inspiration and driven us to action. We founded IMABOX with passion for evoking emotions and creating value. Our small studio is located in Montevideo, Uruguay and we are specialized in developing Architectural Renderings, 3D Architecture, Computer Graphics Images (CGIs) and any kind of Architectural Visualization in the field of architecture, urban planning and landscaping.
